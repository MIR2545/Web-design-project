Industrial Robot

Industrial robots have drastically rationalized many kinds of manufacturing processes in industrial fields. The user interface provided by the robot manufacturer has been almost entirely limited to the so-called teaching pendant.

Augmenting Robots

Augmenting robots either enhance current human capabilities or replace the capabilities a human may have lost. Some examples of augmenting robots are robotic prosthetic limbs or exoskeletons used to lift hefty weights.

Teleoperated robots are mechanical bots controlled by humans. This also allows more direct teleoperation as the user can control the robot with their own motions.These robots usually work in extreme geographical conditions.
